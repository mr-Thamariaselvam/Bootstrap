Welcome to GT Holidays

GT Holidays Pvt. Ltd. is a subsidiary of the Sangam Group of Hotels, one of the largest hotel chains in South Tamil Nadu. Founded in 1968, the Sangam Group has been known for over four decades for its impeccable service and exceptional hospitality, currently operating 19 properties across Tamil Nadu.

Built on this strong foundation, GT Holidays aims to deliver exceptional customer satisfaction and memorable holiday experiences. Whether you're planning a once-in-a-lifetime vacation or an annual corporate retreat, GT Holidays is your go-to travel partner.

As one of the leading destination management companies in South India, we have the experience and infrastructure to meet every customer’s need. Our services include corporate tours, incentive trips, college and school excursions, business travel arrangements, car/railway and hotel bookings, and much more.

Discover what sets GT Holidays apart from the rest—our dedication to excellence and our commitment to making your travel dreams a reality.
